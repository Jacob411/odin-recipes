Grilled Cheese Recipe

Grilled Cheese

Description

This recipe is perfect when you are hungry! It is quick and easy and very tasty.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices-Sliced Bread
  • 2 Tbsp-Butter
  • 2 Tbsp-Mayo
  • 3 slices-American Cheese
  1. Heat a pan on the stove to medium heat
  2. Place butter in pan
  3. Spread mayo onto the sliced bread and place in pan
  4. Place bread into pan
  5. Place american cheese onto bread
  6. Form a snadwich and grill until crispy and golden brown
