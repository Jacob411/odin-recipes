Taco Recipe

Tacos

Description

Tasty ground beef tacos that can be prepared in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz-ground beef
  • 2 Tbsp-taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup-mexican shredded cheese
  • 1 tomato-diced
  • 1/4 head-lettuce
  1. Place ground beef into a pan along with the seasoning
  2. Cook until beef is done
  3. Slice lettuce and tomatoes
  4. Place ingredients into tortillas with cheese and cook until cheese in melted
